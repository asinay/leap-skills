# content-engine
**Go from blank page to 30 days of LinkedIn content ideas in one conversation — all rooted in your real experience.**

Consistency is the only LinkedIn strategy that actually works. This skill removes the "what do I even post about?" block and builds a content calendar from your actual career, not from trending topics.

---

## The Prompt

```
Read my voice-builder and audience-builder profiles if they exist in this project.

I need 30 LinkedIn post ideas rooted in my real experience — not generic tips, not recycled advice. Posts that only I could write.

Ask me these questions first:

1. What are 3-5 topics you could talk about for hours without needing to prepare?
2. What's a mistake you made in your career that taught you something irreplaceable?
3. What's a counterintuitive truth you've learned that took years to figure out?
4. What's a moment from the last 6 months that made you think "this would be a good post"?
5. What questions do people ask you most often — in DMs, in meetings, at events?

After I answer, generate 30 post ideas organized into 5 categories (6 ideas each):

**STORY POSTS** — Real moments from my career, told as narrative
**OPINION POSTS** — Strong take + the experience behind it
**TEACHING POSTS** — Something I know how to do that my audience doesn't yet
**BEHIND-THE-SCENES POSTS** — What my work actually looks like day to day
**CONVERSATION POSTS** — Questions that invite my audience to share their experience

For each idea, give me:
- A working title (not the hook, just what the post is about)
- The hook line (the first sentence that earns "see more")
- The format suggestion: short-form (under 150 words), standard (150-300 words), or long-form (300-500 words)

Flag the 5 strongest ideas with ⭐ — the ones most likely to drive real engagement based on my audience profile.

Then suggest a simple 4-week posting schedule: which type of post on which days, based on what performs best on LinkedIn.
```

---

## How to use your content engine

1. Run this skill once a month
2. Pick your top 8-10 ideas for the next 4 weeks
3. Use **thought-leader-post** or **hook-generator** to turn each idea into a finished draft
4. Batch-write on one day, schedule across the month

**One session per month = consistent presence all month.**
